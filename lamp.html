Lightstick

Most K-pop idol release official lightsticks with specific shapes, colors, and symbols that fans use to show their support during concerts or events.
These lightsticks usually support central control (one uniform color across the venue) or multi-point control (different colors by seat to form patterns or text). Once activated at a venue, the lighting technician controls the color and effects.
